About Me

I’m a German currently based in Providence, Rhode Island — soon moving to New York City. I grew up in Berlin, which gave me a deep love for architecture, a love-hate relationship with grey weather, and a need for green space.

I’m graduating from Brown University in May 2025 with a double major in Environmental Science and Computer Science. My academic path bridges two realizations:

  • Urban environmental spaces are layered with digital networks and cartographic systems that shape how we understand place.
  • Climate-tech solutions are often software-driven — and I want to understand them from the data architecture level up.

I now navigate both of these intersections. Previously, I've had the privilege to:

  • Co-Chair at Better World by Design
  • Teaching Assistant for courses like Sustainable Building Design and Environmental Finance
  • Help develop socially responsible CS curriculum at Brown: CSCI 0111

Incoming: Climate Intelligence Unit @Macquarie Group

Previously:

  • Sustainability in Real Estate Asset Management @ Brookfield
  • Product Design Intern @ BuildingMinds (ClimateTech SaaS)
